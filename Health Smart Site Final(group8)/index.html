About - Health Smart @HK

Welcome to Health Smart Website. In this website. You can get the practical and update information of personal health information, including health eating, healthy body, health care.

Like other region in the world, Hong Kong become increased number of People suffering from noncommunicable disease, which cause ill-health, disability, and premature deaths. With the increasing and ageing population, the burden of noncommunicable disease is expected to increase further. Our goal of this website is going help HK citizen having the healthy lifestyle in smart way.
