Lamma Island Hiking Route
Route： Yung Shue Wan, Lamma Island --> Hung Shing Yeh Beach --> Sok Kwu Wan
Length： About 7 km
Time: About 2 hr
Difficulty： 2 Star (1 Star is Easiest, 5 Star is Hardest)
Starting Point Traffic： Take the Ferry From Central Pier to Yung Shue Wan Pier
Ending Point Traffic Take the Ferry From Sok Kwu Wan to Central Pier
