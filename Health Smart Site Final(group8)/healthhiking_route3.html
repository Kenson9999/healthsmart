龍脊郊遊徑Longji Country Hiking Trail
路線：Route: 土地灣、打爛埕頂山、龍脊、大潭峽、馬塘坳、大浪灣。Tudi Bay, Dabang Chengding Mountain, Longji, Tai Tam Gorge, Matang Au, Big Wave Bay.
長度：Length: 約8.5 公里Approx. 8.5 km
需時：Time required: 約3 小時 (未計休息時間)Approx. 3 hours (excluding breaks)
難度：Difficulty: 3stars
起點交通：Starting point traffic: 於筲箕灣地鐵站搭乘 9 號巴士在土地灣站下車。Take 9 at Shau Kei Wan MTR Station Bus No. and get off at Tudiwan Station.
終點交通：Destination Traffic: 在大浪灣搭乘紅色小巴返回筲箕灣，或沿大浪灣道步出至石澳道口搭乘 9 號巴士返回筲箕灣。Return to Shau Kei Wan by red minibus at Big Wave Bay, or walk along Big Wave Bay Road to Shek O Crossing Bus No. 9 returns to Shau Kei Wan.
