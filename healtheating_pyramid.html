Eating well is important to our physical health. In this website, you will learn the importance of food pyramid, which help us the maintain the balance diet, then food nutrition helps us to know the nutrient classification, finally the food recipe will teach you how to cook the health food by yourselves.
Food pyramid Since different food has different nutrition values, it is not possible to obtain all the nutrients we need form a single food. According food pyramid, we have to eat a variety of foods among all food groups, then each group in order to get different nutrients and meet our daily needs.
Eat most Grain
Eat most vegetables and fruits
Eat moderately meat, fish, egg, dry beans, and milk
Eat less Fat/ Oil, salt, and sugar
Drink adequate amount of fluid everyday (including water, tea, clear soup, etc.)
